How to Play

On a computer: Use the arrow keys to move. Press Space to interact. On a mobile: Use the on-screen joystick to move. Use the interact button to attempt to grab items. Walk Nana around the museum. Move up to an artwork or floor item and attempt to grab it. Each item triggers a question. Get it right and you steal it. Get it wrong and it greys out and you get a strike. Three strikes brings in the guard. If you make it to the exit, you escape with your haul. Questions only get used once until you reset your progress.
