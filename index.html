How to Play

Move around the museum, walk up to an artwork, and attempt to grab it.

Each item triggers a question. Get it right and you steal it. Get it wrong and it greys out and you get a strike.

Three strikes brings in the guard.

On computer, use the arrow keys and press space or E to interact.

On mobile, use the on-screen joystick and the INTERACT button.
