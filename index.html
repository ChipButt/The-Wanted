How to play

  • Move with WASD or arrow keys.
  • On mobile, use the on-screen controls.
  • Walk up to a painting and press E or tap INTERACT.
  • Correct answer: painting stolen and cash added.
  • Wrong answer: painting locks for this run and you gain a strike.
  • At 3 strikes, the guard chases you out.
  • You can leave through the main door any time to bank your haul.
