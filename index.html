How to Play

Move Nana around the museum. Walk up to an artwork or floor item and attempt to grab it. Each item triggers a question. Get it right and you steal it. Get it wrong and it greys out, and you get a strike. Three strikes brings the guard in. If you make it to the exit, you escape with your haul. Questions only get used once until you reset your progress.
